Revolutionary E‑commerce Beauty Platform

Volition is a Kickstarter-like platform for beauty products. Resource, where people can submit ideas for new products and vote on existing ones

Website

Team

1 project manager

1 lead engineer

2 engeneers

Timeline

2015 — ongoing

Location

United States, California

Challenge

The founder of Volition came to Rubyroid Labs by recommendation of her friend with a purpose to create an open and transparent collaboration platform where every beauty product will be created by community. Platform, which meets all modern requirements for web applications — responsiveness, stability and adaptivity.

Custom Web Application Marketing AI Integration System for Order and Delivery Management Voting System Payment System
We had to build a website that could handle the data on the backend, help us put the products up for voting, and aggregate the votes. The concept behind our company is that we only launch products that our community has submitted and chosen. We're a crowd-sourced beauty platform in that sense.

Patrisia Santos

CEO Volition Beauty

Solution

Since the whole process is heavily based on the customer experience, it was extremely important to create not only efficient, but also user-friendly solution. To launch and test the innovative approach in marketing that Volition suggests, it was decided to split the development in 2 phases.

In the first phase our company delivered Model-View-Presenter.

In the second phase we developed major features like:

—  Voting system

—  Products promotion system

—  Social integration

—  Customers reviews aggregation system

—  Retention Science marketing automation

We developed a web solution based on a popular library for e-commerce applications - Spree Commerce. This choice allowed us to implement the solution as fast as possible. Starting to build beta version in May, Volition were able to launch the voting part in July, and the most important, e-commerce section, in October.

Since successful start in 2015, customer has the fully-functioning e-commerce system with a number of great features, that helps thousands of people to take part in the creation of beauty products that they really want.

Results

Today, Volition is a modern and efficient e-Commerce beauty platform. Its fresh layout matches its fresh approach to the way the goods are made and sold. Social integration has already brought more than 100.000 satisfied users and community around the Company is extremely strong and supportive. In August 2016, Volition had a television network debut in a full-hour show. The station reaches more than million households, so Volition went from being a startup to a nationally-distributed company within months. Rubyroid made sure that everything would work well on the site and that platform could handle the inbound traffic. It works flawlessly with no major issues for more than two years.

Nowadays, customer has the fully-functioning e-commerce system with a number of great features, that helps a thousands of people to take part in creation of that beauty products that they really want.
